Research

I'm drawn to the seam where control theory meets learning — systems that carry formal safety guarantees yet stay empirically fast on real hardware.

Current questions: how do we learn the parameters of an event-triggered controller without breaking its stability guarantees? How do fleets of real robots coordinate in real time under conflict?
